# B2C property audience research — working draft

Date: 2026-07-20  
Market scope: Istanbul, Ankara, Izmir and Antalya  
Business constraint: SmartKey is an intermediary/advisory brand and does not own the listed properties.

## Evidence baseline

- Türkiye recorded 1,688,910 house sales in 2025, up 14.3% year over year. Istanbul, Ankara and Izmir led total sales. Foreign-buyer sales were 21,534, down 9.4%, representing 1.3% of all house sales. Istanbul and Antalya led foreign-buyer sales. Source: [TURKSTAT, House Sales Statistics, December 2025](https://veriportali.tuik.gov.tr/Bulten/Index?dil=2&p=House-Sales-Statistics-December-2025-58350).
- Legal ownership transfers are completed through land-registry directorates; preliminary contracts alone do not transfer title. Buyers should verify mortgages, liens and similar restrictions before beginning the transfer process. Source: [Investment Office of the Presidency of Türkiye](https://www.invest.gov.tr/en/InvestmentGuide/Pages/acquiring-property-and-citizenship.aspx).
- Foreign buyers do not need a residence permit as a precondition to acquire eligible real estate, but nationality and location restrictions can apply. The official guide also identifies required land-registry documents and mandatory earthquake insurance for buildings. Source: [Investment Office property guide](https://www.invest.gov.tr/en/InvestmentGuide/Pages/acquiring-property-and-citizenship.aspx).
- The official guide currently describes an exceptional-citizenship route involving qualifying real estate of at least USD 400,000 and a three-year resale restriction. This is legally sensitive, can change, and must never be presented as a guarantee. It requires current professional verification before publication or lead advice.

## Primary audience hypotheses

These are hypotheses for validation through interviews and lead data, not demographic facts.

### 1. Cross-border lifestyle buyer

Goal: a home for relocation, seasonal use or family access to Türkiye.

Key questions:

- Which neighborhoods fit daily life, schools, hospitals and transport?
- What is the true all-in cost beyond the asking price?
- Can a foreign national legally acquire this specific property?
- Who verifies title, restrictions, earthquake insurance and handover?

Trust requirements:

- explicit disclosure that SmartKey is not the property owner;
- verified listing status and last-checked date;
- map, neighborhood context and realistic travel times;
- independent legal/technical due-diligence recommendation;
- clear fee and commission disclosure before commitment.

### 2. Investment and income buyer

Goal: capital preservation, rental income or future resale.

Key questions:

- What assumptions support the projected return?
- Are occupancy, maintenance, tax, currency and management costs included?
- Is the property liquid for the intended buyer segment?
- Is the listing suitable for long-term or short-term rental under current rules?

Trust requirements:

- scenario ranges instead of guaranteed ROI;
- source and date for every market claim;
- separation of asking price, comparable evidence and forecast;
- visible risk section;
- downloadable fact sheet with revision date.

### 3. Residency/citizenship-motivated buyer

Goal: explore whether a purchase can support an immigration objective.

Key questions:

- Does this property and transaction qualify under current rules?
- What valuation, title annotation and holding requirements apply?
- Which claims require a licensed lawyer or government confirmation?

Trust requirements:

- no guarantee language;
- current official source and review date;
- mandatory legal-review handoff;
- clear distinction among property purchase, residence eligibility and citizenship eligibility.

### 4. Remote comparison buyer

Goal: shortlist properties before traveling to Türkiye.

Key questions:

- Is the listing current and represented accurately?
- Can the advisor provide a live video tour and verified documents?
- How can several cities and properties be compared consistently?

Trust requirements:

- standardized property fields and comparison tool;
- media provenance and capture date;
- availability status and last verification date;
- remote viewing and inquiry workflow;
- response-time expectation without false urgency.

## City intent model

City pages should not claim that one city is universally “best.” They should help users compare intent:

- Istanbul: depth of inventory, urban connectivity and the largest foreign-buyer volume in the official 2025 data.
- Antalya: lifestyle/seasonal interest and the second-largest foreign-buyer volume in the official 2025 data.
- Ankara: domestic-market scale, capital-city services and long-term living considerations.
- Izmir: coastal metropolitan lifestyle and domestic-market scale.

Every city page needs dated sources, neighborhood-level nuance and a “who this may fit / may not fit” section.

## Proposed B2C journey

1. Discover through a city, guide, article or listing.
2. Understand SmartKey's intermediary role and verification policy.
3. Filter by city, purpose, property type, price and readiness/status.
4. Compare standardized facts and risks.
5. Request details, remote viewing or consultation.
6. Qualify intent, timing, budget, nationality-related constraints and preferred contact channel.
7. Provide human follow-up and route legal/technical questions to qualified professionals.
8. Record consent, source and outcome for measurement.

## Property page minimum evidence

- listing reference and last verified date;
- city, district and approximate/precise map policy;
- price, currency, status and fee disclosure;
- property type, gross/net area labels, rooms, completion/readiness and title status where verified;
- media source and date;
- amenities separated from nearby services;
- intermediary/non-owner disclosure;
- risk and due-diligence notice;
- source-aware inquiry form;
- no guaranteed return, residence or citizenship claims.

## Recommended CTA hierarchy

Primary: **Request verified details**  
Secondary: **Book a consultation**  
Tertiary: **Compare properties** / **Request a remote viewing**

Avoid generic “Buy now,” false countdowns, unverified scarcity and guaranteed-investment language.
