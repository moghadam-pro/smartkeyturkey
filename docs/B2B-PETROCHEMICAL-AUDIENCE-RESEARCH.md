# B2B petrochemical audience and RFQ research

Date: 2026-07-20

## Objective

Create a credible English-language product discovery and quotation journey for approximately 100 petrochemical products. SmartKey Turkey is presented as a sourcing and coordination intermediary, not as the manufacturer, stock owner, laboratory, carrier or regulatory authority.

## Priority audiences

### Procurement manager

- Needs a comparable quotation, dependable lead time and a clear commercial contact.
- Filters by product, grade, quantity, destination, packaging and Incoterm.
- Expects supplier identity and commercial terms to be confirmed before commitment.

### Technical buyer or production manager

- Needs grade-specific properties, application fit, test method and current documentation.
- Looks for TDS, SDS and, where applicable, a batch Certificate of Analysis.
- Must be able to attach an existing specification or RFQ.

### Import, logistics or compliance manager

- Needs origin, HS/GTIP code, UN number and dangerous-goods classification when applicable.
- Checks packaging, transport mode, destination port, customs requirements and document availability.
- Requires current shipment-specific details rather than generic marketing claims.

### Distributor or trader

- Often requests multiple grades, repeat volumes and destination-specific terms.
- Needs a fast qualification response and a clear distinction between indicative and confirmed availability.

## Core buyer questions

1. Is the exact grade available for the requested quantity and delivery window?
2. Who is the confirmed supplier or manufacturer?
3. Which current TDS, SDS, compliance documents and batch documents are available?
4. What packaging, minimum order and Incoterm can be offered?
5. Is the product classified as dangerous goods, and what transport restrictions apply?
6. What is included in the quotation and how long is it valid?
7. What information will SmartKey coordinate, and what must be verified by the supplier or buyer?

## Recommended inquiry journey

1. Browse or search by product family, application, industry or grade.
2. Review a concise product record with document availability and verification status.
3. Select **Request Quote** with the product and grade prefilled.
4. Submit company, destination, quantity, packaging, Incoterm, date and technical requirements.
5. SmartKey validates completeness and requests missing qualification details.
6. The relevant supplier confirms specifications, availability, price and commercial terms.
7. Buyer receives a traceable quotation and next-step contact.

## RFQ qualification fields

### Required

- Full name
- Business email
- Company name
- Destination country or market
- Product name or grade
- Quantity and unit
- Delivery destination or port
- Application and technical requirements
- Privacy consent

### Optional but valuable

- Phone or WhatsApp
- Packaging
- Preferred Incoterm
- Target delivery date
- Requested documents
- Attached specification or RFQ

## Product taxonomy recommendation

- Product family: polymers, aromatics, olefins, intermediates, industrial chemicals, solvents, glycols and other products
- Polymer type: LDPE, HDPE, PP, PVC, masterbatch or other
- Industry: packaging, pipes, construction, automotive, textile, agriculture, paint/coatings, detergents, cosmetics, pharmaceutical or other
- Application: grade-specific end use
- Physical form: granule, powder, liquid, gas or other
- Document availability: TDS, SDS, CoA, origin and compliance documents
- Supply status: inquiry only, indicative availability, supplier-confirmed or unavailable

The initial categories are a flexible starting point, not a claim that SmartKey supplies every category. Petkim's official catalog demonstrates the usefulness of separating polymers, aromatics, olefins and other products and publishing grade-level TDS/SDS information.

## Trust and claims policy

- Never state that SmartKey manufactures, owns or holds stock unless a specific fact is documented and approved.
- Do not publish live price, availability, origin or delivery time as confirmed without supplier evidence and a freshness date.
- Treat technical values as grade-specific; always identify the source document and revision date.
- Do not infer regulatory compliance from a generic product name.
- Label supplier/manufacturer, document source, verification status and last reviewed date.
- Display: "SmartKey Turkey facilitates sourcing and coordination. Product specifications, availability, pricing, regulatory suitability and final terms are confirmed by the relevant supplier and must be independently reviewed by the buyer."

## Evidence informing the model

- Petkim's official product catalog separates polymer and chemical families and provides grade-level TDS/compliance links.
- Petkim publishes SDS records separately, reinforcing the need to model SDS availability as structured data.
- UNECE ADR 2025 requires classification, packaging, labelling and transport documentation controls for dangerous goods transported by road.
- Türkiye's Ministry of Trade notes that import release depends on trade-policy measures, import formalities and applicable taxes; customs treatment is product-specific.
