# News, laws and events editorial workflow

## Categories

- **Market News** — official statistics and market developments relevant to property or B2B audiences.
- **Laws & Guidance** — general explanations grounded in official rules and guides; never presented as legal advice.
- **Events** — upcoming or completed events with a clear relevance to SmartKeyTurkey audiences.

## Required metadata

Every managed article should include a primary source URL, source name, last-reviewed date and editorial status. AI-assisted articles remain marked `pending human review` until the site owner approves them.

## Publication checks

1. Prefer a primary official source.
2. Record the publication/reference date and distinguish it from the SmartKeyTurkey publication date.
3. Avoid unsupported forecasts, prices or legal conclusions.
4. Keep time-sensitive details such as venue, registration, ticketing and rules linked to the current official source.
5. Add a specific Rank Math description and one descriptive H1.
6. Confirm the article and category pages work in both system color schemes and at mobile width.
7. Revisit dated market statistics when the next official release appears.
